Slide - 01

  1. Gather all the ingredients needed for the sandwich.
  2. Preheat a skillet or grill pan over medium heat.

Time : 5:00 mins

Slide - 02

  1. Slice the sourdough bread into 1/2 inch slices.
  2. Slice the provolone and mozzarella cheese into thin slices.
  3. Slice the tomato into thin rounds.

Time : 2:00 mins

Slide -03

  1. Drizzle the olive oil over one side of each slice of bread
  2. Sprinkle salt and pepper on the oiled side of the bread.

Time : 2:00 mins

Slide - 04

  1. Place the bread slices, oiled side down, onto the preheated skillet or grill pan.
  2. Cook until the bread is toasted and golden brown, about 2-3 minutes.

Time : 2:00 mins

Slide - 05

  1. Add the provolone cheese to one slice of the toasted bread.
  2. Add the mozzarella cheese to the other slice of the toasted bread.

Time : 2:00 mins

Slide - 06

  1. Top the provolone cheese with sliced tomatoes and fresh basil leaves.
  2. Close the sandwich by placing the slice of bread with mozzarella cheese on top of the other slice.

Time : 2:00 mins

Slide - 07

  1. Press down on the sandwich lightly with a spatula.
  2. Cook for another 1-2 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the sandwich is heated through.
  3. Remove from heat and let cool for 1 minute before serving.
  4. Enjoy your delicious Italian-style cheese sandwich!.

Time : 2:00 mins
