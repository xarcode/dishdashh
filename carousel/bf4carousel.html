Slide - 01

  1. Beat 2 eggs in a bowl.
  2. Add 1/4 cup chopped vegetables, 1 finely chopped green chili, 1/4 tsp red chili powder (optional), 1/4 tsp turmeric powder, and salt and pepper to taste.

Time : 2:00 mins

Slide - 02

  1. Mix well to combine.

Time : 1:00 mins

Slide -03

  1. Heat 1 tbsp of oil in a pan over medium heat.

Time : 1:00 mins

Slide - 04

  1. Place 2 slices of bread in the pan and let it cook for 1-2 minutes.

Time : 1:00 mins

Slide - 05

  1. Pour the egg mixture over the bread slices.

Time : 2:00 mins

Slide - 06

  1. Fold the bread in half to cover the egg mixture and let it cook for 2-3 minutes on each side or until the egg is cooked through.

Time : 1:00 mins

Slide - 07

  1. Remove the bread omelette from the pan and serve hot.
  2. Enjoy your delicious Bread Omelette!

Time : 1:00 mins
