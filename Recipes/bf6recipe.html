Oat Meal

Main Ingredient - Oats, Milk

⭐ ⭐ ⭐ ⭐ ⭐ Rating

8-9 Mins
Easy to Cook

Description :

Oatmeal is a warm and comforting breakfast option that is made from rolled or steel-cut oats, water or milk, and optional flavorings like cinnamon and sweeteners like honey or maple syrup. Oatmeal is a great source of fiber and can be customized with your favorite toppings like fresh fruit, nuts, or seeds.

Ingredients :

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup water or milk (dairy or non-dairy)
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon (optional)
  • 1 tbsp honey or maple syrup (optional)
  • Toppings of your choice: sliced banana, berries, nuts, seeds, etc
